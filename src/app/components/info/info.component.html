Info

GuessTheChar is a daily puzzle game inspired by Wordle.

Players have 6 attempts to guess a video game character using the images and hints provided. Each guess reveals another image and hint. Try to guess the character in as few attempts as possible.

New characters are added daily. If you miss some, view the past games list to guess them all.

View your stats to show off you video game character knowledge.

If you encounter issues or have any questions please contact me via email. If you are reporting a bug or broken feature, please provide browser and device information.
